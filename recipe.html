LET'S MAKE PAVLOVA!

If you want a dessert that contains various flavors such as sweet, sour and also fatty, why not try the pavlova recipe. For your information, preparing this pavlova is not that difficult because the main ingredients needed are only egg whites and sugar. So let's try this easy pavlova recipe :)

I N G R E D I E N T S

  • 4 egg whites

  • 1 cup castor sugar

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  • 1 teaspoon vinegar

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

C R E A M - I N G R E D I E N T S

  • 1 cup whipped cream

  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar

  • Sour fruits (strawberries, grapes and kiwi)

P R E P A R A T I O N - M E T H O D

  1. Make sure the bowl and the spinner are dry and not greasy, keep them clean so that the pavlova is beautiful.
  2. Beat the egg whites until foamy. Then add sugar a little at a time and stir until it thickens and has a horn (I want to know if it's okay or not to turn it upside down and not fall).
  3. Add cornstarch, vanilla essence and vinegar. Stir the mixture again using a spatula instead of a curry spoon. Meanwhile, heat the oven at 150'c for 15 minutes.
  4. Bake at a temperature of 140`c for 1 hour or 1 hour 15 min, after cooking leave it in the slightly open oven until it cools.
  5. Beat whipped cream and icing sugar until fluffy. Layer with whipped cream and decorate with your own creativity.

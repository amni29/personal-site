LET'S MAKE PAVLOVA!

If you want a dessert that contains various flavors such as sweet, sour and also fatty, why not try the pavlova recipe. For your information, preparing this pavlova is not that difficult because the main ingredients needed are only egg whites and sugar. So let's try this easy pavlova recipe :)

I N G R E D I E N T S

  • 4 egg whites

  • 1 cup castor sugar

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  • 1 teaspoon vinegar

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence

C R E A M - I N G R E D I E N T S

  • 1 cup whipped cream

  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar

  • Sour fruits

      • strawberries

      • grapes

      • kiwi
